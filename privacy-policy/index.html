Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 11/08/2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to suzuki-amy.co.uk (“we,” “our,” “us”). We are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal data from you when you create an account on our website. The types of personal data we collect may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Any other information you provide during the account creation process

We also collect information automatically through Google Analytics, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Time zone setting
  • Browser plug-in types and versions
  • Operating system and platform
  • Other technology on the devices you use to access this website

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the data collected for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: To create and manage your account on our website.
  • Marketing: To send you promotional materials and updates about our products and services.
  • Analytics: To analyze website traffic and user behavior via Google Analytics to improve our website and services.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. We use the following types of cookies:

  • Tracking Cookies: To monitor and track user activity on our website.
  • Session Cookies: To maintain your session as you navigate through the website.
  • Marketing Cookies: To tailor and deliver relevant marketing messages to you.

You can manage or disable cookies through your web browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

5. Data Sharing

We share your data with third-party services, such as:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and performance tracking.

We do not sell your personal data to any third parties.

6. Your Rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the following rights:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request that we correct any inaccuracies in your personal data.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal data from our records.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [email protected].

We take reasonable steps to protect the personal data we hold from misuse, loss, and unauthorized access. However, no internet-based site can be 100% secure, and we cannot be held responsible for unauthorized or unintended access that is beyond our control.
